Hi. Welcome to Flamolt.

Discover the exciting world of Fantasy Sports, an online sports game centered around strategy that allows you to assemble a virtual team of actual players competing in actual matches.
Assemble your team to gain points in a live game based on the performance of every member ...

Join or Create a League

Leagues are groups of fantasy teams that compete against each other. You can either join a public league or create a private league with friends or colleagues

Understand Scoring Rules

Familiarize yourself with the rules, including how points are awarded for different player actions (e.g., goals, assists)

Draft Your Team

Participate in a draft to select players for your team. Leagues may use different draft formats, such as a snake draft or auction draft. Pay attention to player positions, team dynamics, and injury reports

Manage Your Team

Once your team is drafted, you'll need to manage it throughout the season. This involves setting your lineup, making trades with other teams, and picking up free agents from the waiver wire

Follow Player Performance

Keep track of how your players are performing in real-life games. Player statistics directly contribute to your fantasy team's score

Score Points

Points are awarded based on the statistical performance of your chosen players. These points accumulate, and your team competes against others in your league

Engage in Head-to-Head or Rotisserie Format

Leagues may follow a head-to-head or rotisserie format. In head-to-head, your team competes against another team each week, while rotisserie involves accumulating stats across various categories throughout the season

Playoffs and Championships

If your league has a playoff format, the top teams will compete for the championship at the end of the season

Enjoy Prizes

Top leagues have entry fees, and winners receive prizes. Make sure to understand the league's rules regarding prizes and payouts.

Interact and Have Fun

Stay engaged, interact with other league members, and have fun following the sport you love

Fantasy sports have transformed the way fans engage with their favorite games, offering an immersive and strategic experience beyond mere spectatorship. In this virtual realm, participants become team managers, handpicking real-life athletes to compose their dream teams. Points are earned based on the actual performance of chosen players in live games, turning every goal, touchdown, or home run into a moment of triumph for fantasy team owners. Whether competing against friends in private leagues or joining public competitions, the excitement lies in the strategic acumen required to navigate player drafts, trades, and lineup decisions. The blend of statistical analysis and sports fandom adds a layer of complexity, creating a dynamic and interactive community. Fantasy sports not only deepen the connection between fans and the games they love but also foster camaraderie and friendly competition among enthusiasts worldwide.
